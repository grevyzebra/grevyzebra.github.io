Relationship between all of your Facebook friends still possible?

Is it still possible to have a personal relationship to all of your Facebook friends ?

Study: Is the number of written messages rising with the number of friends in the friendlist ?

saturation point -> 200 contacts in friendslist
as more friends, the less messages!
over 200 friends: less messages
less 200 friends: more messages

shows that there is just a small part of real personal relationships = people who you write messages with.
The rest of the people is not important enough to write with.

differ between kind of relationships: more personal when you write messages, more unpersonal when they are just friends in the friendlist.

result: There are still relationships to all of your facebook friends, but you have to differ between strong and weak ones.
